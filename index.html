Boreal Shield

Welcome to the second-largest ecozone in Canada - the Boreal Shield! Stretching from Saskatchewan to Newfoundland, this ecozone covers over 1.8 million square kilometres of land (18% of Canadas land mass), and contains distinctive biotic and abiotic features unique to this ecozone. With a great climate, luscious vegetation, fantastic human activities, and diverse wildlife, we hope that you will come and enjoy your stay in the one and only Boreal Shield Ecozone!
